    • quietude in iron

    Back home, I find quiet and calmness in my favorite pastime: weightlifting.

    For me, weightlifting is a way to connect with and strengthen my body—and, in the process, achieve mental tranquility. Over the years, I’ve tried many different workout styles and programs, but I always come back to what I enjoy most: moving heavy weights.
