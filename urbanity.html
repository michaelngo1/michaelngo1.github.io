    • urbanity

    While natural landscapes are unparalleled in their splendor, I am just as fascinated by the built environment and the human activity that exists within it.

    Despite only being a child during my time in NYC—the quintessential big city—that formative experience laid the foundation for my appreciation of the urban environment. And just as each natural landscape is unique, each urban environment is unique— representing the intersection of history, culture, technology, economy, and other fundamental components of civilization.
