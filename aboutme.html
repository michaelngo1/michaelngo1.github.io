    • about me

    Life is a continuous stream of discovery and experiences.

    I am the son of Vietnamese immigrants and a Southern California native (with a small childhood stint in New York City) who hopes to make this world a better place for all of its inhabitants. Professionally, I am a hospital management professional and aspiring data scientist with a Master of Public Health from the University of California, Irvine and a Biology degree from Stanford University. But while I strive to make meaningful impact for others, I also strive to devote time to enrich my own life, and two of my favorite channels for that are traveling and fitness.
