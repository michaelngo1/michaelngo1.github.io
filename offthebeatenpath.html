    • off the beaten path

    My ever-burgeoning interest in nature and fascination with its natural beauty began when I was a kid being paraded around national parks in the Western US by my family and tour groups.

    As the years pass, my appreciation for nature continues to deepen, and my latest trips have me exploring its nooks and crannies more and more deeply. And as I explore these places, I continue to be enthralled by the vastness and the unique and diverse landscapes of our planet. Here’s to preserving these ecosystems for posterity—mankind and otherwise.
